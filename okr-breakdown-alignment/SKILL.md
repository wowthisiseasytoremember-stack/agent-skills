# OKR Breakdown & Alignment

**Purpose:** Align business goals with engineering tasks.

When given a high-level OKR:
- Break it down into 3-4 key initiatives.
- For each initiative, provide a specific success metric.
- Suggest engineering effort (Small/Medium/Large).
- Note any dependencies.
- Conclude with a one-sentence metric that proves overall success.
