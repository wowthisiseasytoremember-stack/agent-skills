# Architecture Explainer

**Purpose:** Explain engineering proposals to executives.

When given an engineering proposal, provide:
1. One ASCII diagram illustrating the before/after or the new architecture.
2. Three executive bullets including:
   - Business benefit
   - Short-term impact (1-week / 1-month)
   - Long-term impact (1-year)
