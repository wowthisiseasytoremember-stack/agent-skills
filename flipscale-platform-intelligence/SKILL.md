# FlipScale Platform Intelligence

The "Strategist" for marketplace optimization. This skill manages the complex, ever-changing fee structures of 50+ marketplaces and helps the user decide where to list items for maximum profit.

## Core Data
- **Fee Matrix**: Centralized knowledge of eBay (12.9%), Poshmark (20%), Mercari (10%), etc.
- **Shipping Estimates**: Factoring in buyer-paid vs. seller-paid shipping across different weights.
- **Tax Nexus**: Logic for calculating estimated tax reserves based on jurisdiction.

## Storyboarding Comparison
When designing the "Where to List" engine:
1.  **Input**: Sale price and item weight.
2.  **Logic**: Side-by-side net profit calculation for Poshmark, eBay, and Mercari.
3.  **Suggestion**: Highlights the "Fastest Flip" vs. "Max Profit" options.

**Honest math.** We don't just show the fee; we show the *actual* amount the user puts in their pocket. Transparency over simplicity.
