# Reseller 3-Statement Financials (Reseller-Speak)

## 1. The "Gas Tank" (Cash Flow Statement)
**Objective:** How much cash is available to buy more inventory right now?
- **Formula:** `Cash on Hand - (Pending Shipping Labels + Outstanding Returns) = Gas Tank`
- **Insight:** If the Gas Tank is < 10% of total capital, sourcing must stop until sales occur.

## 2. The "Scoreboard" (Profit & Loss / P&L)
**Objective:** How much did I actually clear this month?
- **Revenue:** Total Sales (Gross).
- **COGS:** Cost of Goods Sold (Original purchase price of sold items).
- **Fees/Shipping:** Marketplace deductions.
- **Net Profit:** `Revenue - COGS - Fees - Shipping - Business Expenses`.
- **Target:** Net Profit should be > 30% of Gross Revenue.

## 3. The "Stockpile Value" (Balance Sheet)
**Objective:** What is my total worth if I liquidated everything today?
- **Assets:** `Cash + (Current Inventory * Estimated Liquidation Price)`.
- **Liabilities:** `Pending Fees + Unpaid Sourcing Debt`.
- **Net Worth:** `Assets - Liabilities`.
- **Insight:** A growing Stockpile Value indicates a healthy scaling business, provided STR remains stable.
