# FlipScale Behavioral Planner

The "Soul" of FlipScale. This skill focuses on the psychological layer of the app—how it feels to win, and how we motivate the user without predatory patterns.

## Core Concepts
1.  **The Victory Cycle**: Trigger (Real Event) -> Action (Log Sale) -> Reward (Awe Engine) -> Investment (Next Goal).
2.  **Haptic Hierarchy**: Defining "Light" (confirm), "Medium" (save), and "Heavy" (big win) vibrations.
3.  **Win Framing**: Transforming stagnation alerts into "Ready to Liquidate" opportunities.

## Storyboarding Feedback
When planning a reward, consider:
- **Visual**: Does the dashboard glow, pulse, or show confetti?
- **Tactile**: What is the haptic pattern? (e.g., "The Register Drop")
- **Auditory**: Is there a spatial audio chime for God-Tier wins?
- **Copy**: Does the toast say "Nice Flip!" or "Command Center ROI Optimized"?

---

## Iron Law of Magic
**Real events only.** We never manufacture fake dopamine. Every pulse and vibration must be earned by a real financial win.
