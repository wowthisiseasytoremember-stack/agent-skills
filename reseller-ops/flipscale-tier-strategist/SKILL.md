# FlipScale Tier Strategist

The "Conductor" of the adaptive UI. This skill ensures that the lady thirifting once a month and the enterprise operator with multiple storage units both feel like the app was built specifically for them.

## The 4-Tier Matrix
1.  **Closet Casual**: Playful, encouraging, focus on "Fun Money."
2.  **Shelf Hustler**: Energetic, focused on "Hustle Momentum."
3.  **Garage Pro**: Professional, data-driven, focused on "Operational ROI."
4.  **Storage Empire**: Clinical, enterprise-grade, focused on "System Throughput."

## Adaptive Invariants
- **Copy**: Casual gets "Nice score!", Empire gets "+$200 (target)."
- **Metrics**: Casual sees 3 cards, Empire sees the full Command Center grid.
- **Complexity**: Hide advanced features (like freight tracking) from lower tiers to prevent overwhelm.

---

## Iron Law of Tiers
**Self-selection over gating.** We don't block features; we adapt the focus. The user can change their "Identity" anytime to see more or less data.
