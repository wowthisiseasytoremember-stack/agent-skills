# Reseller Unit Economics

## Overview
This skill adapts Private Equity-style portfolio monitoring for the reselling ecosystem. It transforms a simple list of inventory into a high-fidelity "Investment Portfolio" with deep KPI insights to maximize capital efficiency and IRR (Internal Rate of Return).

## Workflow: Portfolio Health Audit
1. **STR Calculation**: Measure Sell-Through Rate (`Sold / Listed * 100`) for the current period (Daily/Monthly/YTD).
2. **Velocity Audit**: Calculate `WADTS` (Weighted Average Days to Sell) to identify "Trapped Capital" in slow categories.
3. **Category Profitability**: Rank inventory categories (e.g., Clothing, Shoes, Vintage) by `Net Margin %`.
4. **ROI per Item vs. Portfolio ROI**: Compare individual "Home Runs" (Unicorn Flips) against the aggregate portfolio health.

## Guidelines
- **Focus on Velocity (IRR)**: A 50% ROI in 2 days is superior to a 200% ROI in 6 months due to higher capital rotation.
- **Identify Negative Carry**: Items with zero sales but storage/platform fees create "Negative Carry" and must be prioritized for liquidation.
- **Double-Down on High-STR Categories**: Use data to shift sourcing focus toward categories with the highest sell-through, even if margins are lower.
