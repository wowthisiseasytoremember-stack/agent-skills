# Reseller Portfolio-Level Unit Economics (PE-Style)

## Key KPIs for Resellers (from Private Equity Plugin)

### 1. Sell-Through Rate (STR)
`STR = (Sold Items in Period / Total Items Listed) * 100`
- **Target:** 40% (Casual) / 60% (Pro).
- **Insight:** Low STR indicates overpricing or poor inventory selection.

### 2. Weighted Average Days to Sell (WADTS)
`WADTS = Σ (Sold Date - Purchase Date) / Count of Sold Items`
- **Insight:** High WADTS increases "trapped capital" and decreases IRR (Internal Rate of Return).

### 3. Net Margin per Category
`Category Margin = (Category Gross Profit / Category Revenue) * 100`
- **Action:** Double down on categories (e.g., Vintage, Shoes) with > 40% margin. Liquidate categories with < 15% margin.

### 4. Capital Efficiency
`Efficiency = Gross Profit / Total Invested (COG-S)`
- **Insight:** $1.00 invested should return $2.50+ in revenue (ROI of 150%+).

## Portfolio Monitoring
- **Sourcing Strategy:** Focus on items with high ROI *and* low WADTS.
- **Inventory Health:** Audit "Stale" inventory categories periodically (e.g., monthly).
- **Market Sensitivity:** Adjust pricing based on STR trends per marketplace.
