# Design-to-Engineering Handoff Checklist

**Purpose:** Ensure design specs are complete before coding starts.

When given a design mockup or description:
- Validate states (empty, loading, error, success).
- Check edge cases (long text, mobile responsiveness, dark mode).
- Confirm interactions (click, hover, scroll).
- Ensure accessibility hints (keyboard nav, screen readers) are documented.

Return: 'Ready to hand to eng' or 'Design needs 1 more pass'.
