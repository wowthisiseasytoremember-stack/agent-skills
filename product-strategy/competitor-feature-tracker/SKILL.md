# Competitor Feature Tracker

**Purpose:** Track and respond to competitor updates.

When given a list of competitor features:
- Rank each as 'Must-have', 'Nice-to-have', or 'Meh'.
- Assign a strategic threat score (1-5).
- Estimate the engineering lift (1-5).
- Suggest the appropriate competitive response.
