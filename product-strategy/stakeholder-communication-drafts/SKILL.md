# Stakeholder Communication Drafts

**Purpose:** Generate tailored project updates for different audiences.

When provided with raw project data, generate three versions:
- 1 Executive summary (2 bullets, tone: confident)
- 1 Customer-facing announcement (friendly, no jargon)
- 1 Internal team update (honest, including risks)
