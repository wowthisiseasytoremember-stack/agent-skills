# Launch Readiness Checklist

**Purpose:** Ensure no pre-launch item is missed.

When provided with a launch target, give a Go/No-Go checklist grouped by:
- Legal & compliance
- Performance
- Customer communication
- Rollback plan

Each item must be one line and highly scannable.
