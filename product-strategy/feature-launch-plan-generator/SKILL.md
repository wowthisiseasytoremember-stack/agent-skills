# Feature Launch Plan Generator

**Purpose:** Ensure a smooth feature rollout.

When given a feature and launch date:
- List Pre-launch tasks (1 week before) with owners.
- List Launch day checklist items with timing.
- List Post-launch monitoring tasks (1 week) and what to watch.
- Define the rollback procedure in one paragraph.
