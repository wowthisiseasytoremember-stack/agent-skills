# Pricing & Packaging Strategy Advisor

**Purpose:** Test pricing strategies before deployment.

When given a pricing proposal:
- Identify target customer personas.
- Assess cannibalization risk of existing plans.
- Estimate revenue impact.
- Flag positioning risks (market confusion).
- Recommend: proceed, test, or rethink.
