# Meeting-to-Task Converter

**Purpose:** Convert meeting notes to actionable tickets.

When given a transcript or raw meeting notes, convert them into Jira-ready tickets.
For each ticket, include:
- Title
- Description
- Owner (if discernible, or 'Unassigned')
- Due date (default to one week if unspecified)
- Suggested Label(s)
