# Customer Feedback Analyzer

**Purpose:** Analyze raw feedback into actionable themes.

When given raw support tickets, reviews, or survey responses:
1. Cluster them by theme.
2. Count the frequency of each.
3. Assign a priority ranking (P0/P1/P2).
4. Provide a one-line estimate for a potential fix.
