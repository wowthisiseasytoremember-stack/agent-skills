# Burn-Down & Velocity Translator

**Purpose:** Report sprint health to leadership.

When given sprint metrics (velocity, completed stories, blockers, scope creep):
- Translate the numbers into an executive-friendly narrative.
- Clearly answer: "Are we on track?"
- Identify the real risks to project success.
